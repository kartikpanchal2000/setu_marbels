About Us

We’re a family run business and share a mutual affection to stone designs, carvings and decor of antique, vintage, heritage, French and mid-century classics. Armed with our knowledge of stone quarrying, cutting, carving and fitting, we travel cities and villages throughout the map to discover and draft items of desire to share with our unique and stylish customers from all over the globe With our creative team of interior designers, we innovate new designs and manufacture them to reality. Our bespoke stone decor service allow you to customise your decor from scratch.
